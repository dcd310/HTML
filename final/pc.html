Top PC Games

5. Counter Strike: Global Offensive

Counter Strike: Global Offensive(more commonly known as CSGO) is a multiplayer first-person shooter game and stands as the fourth game in the Counter Strike gaming family. CSGO sold over 12 million copies and was the best-selling game when it was released for windows, OS X, Xbox 360, and PS3 in 2012. As the newest game on this list, CSGO has gained a surprising amount of players over the few years it has been live for. Today, there is a total of 250 million hours of gameplay and an average of 350 thousand active players per day.

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is an online shooter game developed by Hidden Path Entertainment and Valve Corporation.

4. Dota 2

Dota 2 is currently the most played game on steam with an active fanbase of 650 thousand players. When released three years ago, it quickly rose to be the most popular Valve game ever created.Dota 2, as the name might suggest, is the second game of the series Defense of the Ancients(DotA). It is a free-to-play multiplayer online battle arena(MOBA) that requires harsh in-game micro and macro mechanics.

1. League of Legends

League of Legends has been dominant in the online gaming scene since 2012 when it averaged 12million players a day. Now, the game has propelled to over 70 million players a month who total over one billion hours. Such a big game is also included with a huge pro-gaming scene. Each year there is a worlds championship where teams across the world compete against each other. The winner takes home a huge prize pool that is usually generated by fans buying in-game skins or donations.
