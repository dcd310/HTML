Derrick's Favorites

  • PC Games
  • MMORPG Games
  • iPhone Games

PC: League of Legends

I've been playing League since season 3 and have liked it due to the mechanical prowess required to be good at the game. It's an interactive game that requires you to work with teammates in order to beat the enemy team.

MMORPG: MapleStory

Maplestory makes it to the top on my MMORPG list due to nostalgic feelings. I've played maplestory ever since I was young and grew to love the party play and cute monsters it offered.

Phone: Puzzle & Dragons

Puzzle & Dragons (PAD) is a highly mechanical game that requires one to have lots of skills. The player has to move and match colored orbs that would produce different game interactions. The most similar game to PAD is Bejeweled, but, of course, Bejeweled is just a simplified version.
